About Me

Hi, I'm Unish Dhungana, a cybersecurity and ethical hacking enthusiast currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Information Technology at Phoenix College of Management, Maitidevi, Kathmandu.

My passion for cybersecurity stems from my deep interest in understanding how technology works and my attention to security details. Fascinated by the negative impacts of technology when exploited, I aim to be a strong defense against cyber threats. My favorite area within this field is ethical hacking, where I thrive on identifying vulnerabilities and strengthening systems.

So far, I've gained knowledge in Networking and explored tools like Wireshark (though it was challenging!). I also have experience with Python, but I'm working on advancing my scripting skills. While I haven't obtained certifications yet, I'm actively building my expertise and hands-on experience.

My future goals include diving deeper into penetration testing, mastering tools like Nmap, and building a strong foundation in network security. I am dedicated to continuous learning and growing my skills to contribute to a safer digital world.
